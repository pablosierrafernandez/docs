# Analytics Dashboard

The **[Analytics Dashboard](https://sandbox.heify.com/sandbox/analytics)** provides powerful, AI-driven insights from your transcription data. Analyze trends, discover patterns, identify anomalies, and gain a deep understanding of your audio data at scale.


<Info>
  Analytics are generated for transcriptions processed under a **specific configuration**.
</Info>

### Synthesis

AI-powered summaries to understand your data at a glance.

- **Executive Summary**: Highlight key findings, temporal trends, and opportunities or risks for decision-making.

- **Qualitative Summary**: Identify recurring themes, issues, and keywords in the audios based on the qualitative analysis of textual fields.

---

### General Distribution

Fundamental metrics about your audio files.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Duration">
    #### Audio Duration Distribution
    
    **Displays:**
    - **Min**: Shortest audio file length
    - **Average**: Mean duration across all files
    - **Max**: Longest audio file length
    - **Bar chart**: Distribution histogram showing frequency of different duration ranges
    
    **Insights:**
    - Identify typical conversation length
    - Spot unusually long/short calls
    - Understand variance in your data
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Speakers">
    #### Speakers Distribution
    
    **Displays:**
    - **Average speakers** per audio file
    - **Donut chart**: Percentage breakdown by number of speakers
    
    **Insights:**
    - Understand conversation complexity
    - Identify one-on-one vs. group conversations
    - Validate speaker detection accuracy
    
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Languages">
    #### Language Distribution
    
    **Displays:**
    - **Pie chart**: Percentage of transcriptions by detected language
    
    **Insights:**
    - Understand your customer base geographic distribution
    - Plan multilingual support strategies
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

---

### Quality & Confidence

Assess the completeness and reliability of your extracted data.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Completeness">
    #### Data Completeness
    
    For each `extraction_field`, a progress bar shows the percentage of transcriptions where a valid (non-null) value was successfully extracted.
    
    **Use this to:**
    - Identify which fields are reliably extracted
    - Find fields that need description improvements
    - Understand data gaps in your conversations
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Audios to Review">
    #### Transcriptions Requiring Review
    
    A table listing transcriptions with a high number of missing extraction fields.
    
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Anomalies">
    #### Anomaly Detection
    
    Highlights transcriptions where an extracted value significantly deviates from the average.
    
   
    **Use cases:**
    - **Fraud detection**: Unusually high transaction amounts
    - **Quality issues**: Extremely low ratings requiring investigation
    - **Outlier analysis**: Edge cases for special handling
    
    <Warning>
      Anomalies aren't always errors. They might represent legitimate edge cases that need attention and review.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

---

#### Extraction Fields Analysis

Detailed, field-by-field breakdown of your extracted data.
Each extraction field gets its own card with visualizations tailored to its data type.


<Tabs>
  <Tab title="String & Array Fields">
    #### Text and List Fields
    
    For `string` and `array` type fields (e.g., "Category", "Mentioned Products").
    
    **Value Frequency Chart**
    
    A bar chart showing the most common values and their counts.
    
    **Insights:**
    - Identify most common categories/topics
    - Prioritize product features to develop
    - Understand customer pain points
    - Track trends in conversation topics
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Number Fields">
    #### Numerical Fields
    
    For `number` type fields (e.g., "Rating", "Price", "Age").
    
    **Distribution Histogram**
    
    A bar chart showing the frequency of values in different ranges.
    
    **Insights:**
    - Understand value distribution (normal, skewed, bimodal)
    - Identify common ranges
    - Spot outliers visually
    - Track central tendency
    
    **Use cases:**
    - Pricing analysis
    - Quality score tracking
    - Customer age demographics
    - Transaction value patterns
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Boolean Fields">
    #### True/False Fields
    
    For `boolean` type fields (e.g., "Issue Resolved", "Recommended").
    
    **Displays**
    
    **Donut Chart** showing the proportion of `True` vs. `False` values.
    
    **Insights:**
    - Track success rates
    - Monitor KPIs (resolution rate, satisfaction, etc.)
    - Identify improvement opportunities
    
    **Use cases:**
    - Support ticket resolution rates
    - Customer satisfaction (yes/no)
    - Product recommendation likelihood
    - Compliance (terms accepted yes/no)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Temporal Analysis

Understand how your data evolves over time.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Trend Chart">
    #### Time-Based Trends
    
    A multi-axis chart plotting the evolution of metrics over time.
    
    **Displays:**
    - **Line charts**: Numerical field averages over time
    - **Bar chart**: Volume of transcriptions per time period
    - **Multiple Y-axes**: Different scales for different numerical extraction fields metrics
    
    **Customization:**
    - Select which fields to display
    - Choose time granularity (daily, weekly, monthly)
    - Filter date range
    
    **Insights:**
    - Track improvement over time
    - Correlate volume with quality
    - Identify seasonal patterns
    - Measure impact of changes (new features, policy updates)
    
    **Use cases:**
    - Monitor customer satisfaction trends
    - Track support quality improvements
    - Seasonal demand analysis
    - A/B testing results validation
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Activity Heatmap">
    #### Time-of-Day & Day-of-Week Patterns
    
    A visual grid showing the volume of transcriptions by:
    - **Rows**: Days of the week (Monday-Sunday)
    - **Columns**: Hours of the day (0-23)
    - **Color intensity**: Number of transcriptions
    
    **Insights:**
    - Identify peak hours for customer interactions
    - Optimize staff scheduling
    - Understand customer behavior patterns
    - Plan maintenance windows for low-traffic times
    
    **Use cases:**
    - **Support teams**: Schedule more agents during peak hours
    - **Sales**: Know when leads are most active
    - **Operations**: Plan system updates during low-traffic periods
  </Tab>
  
</Tabs>

### Relationships & Patterns

Discover hidden connections between data points.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Correlations">
    #### Numerical Field Correlations
    
    A heatmap matrix visualizing the correlation between all numerical extraction fields.
    
    **Heatmap colors:**
    - **Strong positive** (blue): Values move together (0.7 to 1.0)
    - **Weak/no correlation** (white): No relationship (-0.3 to 0.3)
    - **Strong negative** (red): Values move oppositely (-1.0 to -0.7)
    
    
    **Interpretation:**
    - `Price ↔ Duration`: 0.68 = Strong positive correlation
      - Longer conversations → Higher prices
    - `Rating ↔ Duration`: -0.05 = No correlation
      - Conversation length doesn't affect rating
    
    
   **Use cases:**
    - Identify factors that impact key metrics
    - Understand causation vs. correlation
    - Validate hypotheses
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Co-occurrence">
    #### Array Field Co-occurrence
    
    For `array` type fields (e.g., "Pros", "Cons", "Features Mentioned"), this shows how frequently two items appear together.
    
    **Heatmap matrix:**
    
    Shows the number or percentage of transcriptions where both items were mentioned.
    
    **Use cases:**
    - **Product bundling**: Package frequently co-occurring features
    - **Marketing**: Create messaging around common pairs
    - **Sales training**: Educate reps on related features
    - **Development**: Prioritize integrated feature development
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
