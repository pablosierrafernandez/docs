# Sandbox Access

The Sandbox login page is your entry point to Heify's visual platform. You'll need valid credentials to access all features including API key management, configuration building, and transcription analysis.

## Login

The authentication interface provides a simple, secure entry to your Heify account.

<Info>
  If you don't have an account yet, [contact with us](https://www.heify.com/contact.html) to request access.
</Info>


If you're logging in for the first time, you'll receive a **temporary password** via email.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Your Email">
    Look for a welcome email from Heify with your temporary password.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Use your email and the temporary password on the login page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Change Password">
    You'll be prompted to create a new, permanent password.
    
    **Password Requirements:**
    - Minimum 8 characters
    - At least one uppercase letter
    - At least one lowercase letter
    - At least one number
    - At least one special character
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Granted">
    After setting your new password, you'll be redirected to the Sandbox dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
